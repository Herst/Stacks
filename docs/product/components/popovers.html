{% header h2 | Examples %}

Popovers can be presented in any direction you choose. Popover modifiers are named by the direction in which they’ll appear. The primary direction (top, right, bottom, left) will appear first, followed by the secondary (center(default), top, right, bottom, left). For example, a popover with .popover__bottom applied will appear centered below its originating element. .popover__top-right will appear above the element and to the right.

Popover appears to the right of the trigger centered vertically.

.s-popover__right-top

Popover appears to the right of the trigger aligned to the top vertically.

.s-popover__right-bottom

.s-popover__left

Popover appears to the left of the trigger centered vertically.

.s-popover__left-top

Popover appears to the left of the trigger aligned to the top vertically.

.s-popover__left-bottom
